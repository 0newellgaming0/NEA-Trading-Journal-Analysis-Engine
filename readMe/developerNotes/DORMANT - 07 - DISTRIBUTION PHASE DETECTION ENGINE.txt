DISTRIBUTION PHASE DETECTION ENGINE (INSTITUTIONAL FIXED)
1. Overview

The Distribution Phase Detection Engine is a market structure analytics module designed to identify institutional selling behavior at the end of an uptrend. It models how large participants (institutions, funds, liquidity providers) exit positions through:

Trend exhaustion
Volatility expansion
Volume climax behavior
Structural breakdown (lower highs)
Momentum deterioration

This module is built around Wyckoff-style distribution logic, enhanced with quantitative slope analysis, volatility comparison, and volume pressure scoring.

Its primary output is a classification of market state into:

DISTRIBUTION (confirmed institutional exit)
TRANSITION (early warning / weakening structure)
NONE (no meaningful distribution behavior detected)
2. Core Philosophy

This engine is based on a simplified institutional auction model:

Price rises during accumulation → stalls during distribution → breaks during markdown

Distribution is identified not by a single indicator, but by confluence of structural weakening signals:

Trend maturity
Momentum decay
Volatility expansion after compression
Volume climax behavior
Failure to form higher highs

The system assumes:

Institutions do not exit randomly
Exit behavior leaves structural footprints
Those footprints are measurable using slope, range, and volume dynamics

5. Structural Windows

The engine evaluates price behavior across three rolling windows:

Window	Purpose
recent (-10)	Immediate market behavior
mid (-20:-10)	Transition structure
long (-60)	Trend context baseline

These windows are used to compare:

Momentum decay
Volatility expansion
Structural weakness evolution
6. Signal Components
6.1 Trend Maturity
slope_long = np.polyfit(range(30), close[-30:], 1)[0]
trend_strength = abs(slope_long)
trend_mature = trend_strength > np.std(close[-30:]) * 0.10

Meaning:

Measures long-term directional exhaustion
Compares slope stability vs volatility baseline

Interpretation:

Mature trend = late-stage move likely near exhaustion
6.2 Momentum Fading
momentum_fading = slope_recent < slope_mid

Meaning:

Recent slope weaker than prior slope

Interpretation:

Momentum is decaying
Buyers are losing control
6.3 Range Expansion (Volatility Expansion)
recent_range > prior_range * 1.25

Meaning:

Price range expands significantly after contraction

Interpretation:

Institutional distribution often begins with volatility expansion
Breaks prior tight structure
6.4 Volume Distribution Pressure
vol_expansion = volume[-1] > avg_vol * 1.6
vol_climax = volume[-1] > mean(vol[-20:]) * 1.8

Meaning:

Sudden surge in volume at late stage

Interpretation:

Institutional exit activity
Liquidity absorption phase
6.5 Selling Pressure (Rejection Wicks)
upper_wick = np.mean(high - close)
rejection = upper_wick > std(close) * 0.55

Meaning:

Price repeatedly rejected at highs

Interpretation:

Supply absorption at elevated prices
Aggressive selling into strength
6.6 Structural Failure (Lower Highs)
lower_highs = close[-1] < np.max(close[-10:-1])

Meaning:

Current price fails to break prior highs

Interpretation:

Breakdown of bullish structure
First sign of distribution control shift
7. Scoring Model

Each condition contributes to a weighted institutional distribution score:

Signal	Weight
Momentum fading	+0.25
Volume expansion	+0.20
Rejection (wicks)	+0.15
Lower highs	+0.15
Range expansion	+0.15
Trend maturity	+0.10
Final Score Calculation
confidence = min(score, 1.0)
8. Phase Classification Logic
🔴 DISTRIBUTION (Confirmed)
if confidence >= 0.75 and lower_highs and vol_climax

Conditions:

High confidence score
Structural breakdown present
Volume climax confirmed

Meaning:

Institutional distribution is active
Smart money is exiting aggressively
Market is entering markdown risk zone
🟡 TRANSITION (Early Warning)
if confidence >= 0.45

Meaning:

Early distribution signals forming
No full breakdown yet
Market still structurally intact but weakening
🟢 NONE (No Distribution)

Default state when:

Weak scoring
No structural confirmation

Meaning:

No institutional exit behavior detected
Trend is either healthy or neutral
