## OVERVIEW

The Mat Hold module is a continuation-based candlestick pattern engine designed to detect structured trend continuation after an impulsive expansion leg followed by a controlled consolidation phase.

It is built around institutional behavior modeling, where:
- Strong directional impulse candles establish imbalance
- Multi-candle pullbacks represent controlled absorption
- Final breakout confirms continuation of trend dominance

This module is designed for:
- Multi-timeframe trading systems
- Institutional behavior detection frameworks
- Wyckoff-aligned continuation analysis
- Event-driven trade journaling systems

---

## CORE CONCEPT

A Mat Hold structure is defined as:

1. **Impulse Candle (Trend Initiation)**
   - Strong directional candle (bullish or bearish)
   - Establishes price imbalance

2. **Controlled Pullback (Consolidation Phase)**
   - Multiple candles remain within structural bounds
   - Retracement is shallow and non-disruptive
   - No full reversal of impulse move

3. **Continuation Candle (Breakout / Breakdown)**
   - Price resumes original direction
   - Confirms continuation of institutional trend flow

---

## EVENT LIFECYCLE

The module follows a strict event lifecycle:

### 1. SEED
- Early structural formation detected
- Pullback is forming but not complete
- No confirmation yet

### 2. PENDING
- Full Mat Hold structure identified
- Awaiting directional validation

### 3. CONFIRMED
- Price breaks structure boundary
- Continuation validated

### 4. FAILED
- Structure invalidated by reversal
- Opposite boundary violated

### 5. EXPIRED
- No resolution within allowed timeframe (default: 15 bars)

---

## DETECTION LOGIC

### FULL PATTERN DETECTOR

The full detector validates:

#### Impulse Candle
- Body size ≥ 60% of range
- Establishes directional bias

#### Pullback Conditions
Bullish:
- Pullback candles stay above impulse low
- Highs remain below impulse high (controlled compression)
- No deep retracement into impulse body

Bearish:
- Pullback candles stay below impulse high
- Lows remain above impulse low
- No structural break of impulse zone

#### Continuation Candle
Bullish:
- Final candle closes above impulse high

Bearish:
- Final candle closes below impulse low

---

### SEED DETECTOR

The seed detector identifies early-stage structures:
- Detects impulse + partial pullback formation
- Does not require completion candle
- Used for early positioning and monitoring

---

## LOOSENED SYMMETRY MODEL (IMPORTANT EXTENSION)

Unlike strict harmonic patterns, Mat Hold uses **loose symmetry rules**:

### Key Principle:
> Structure matters more than candle uniformity

### Allowed Variability:
- Pullback candle sizes may vary
- No requirement for equal-bodied candles
- No strict Fibonacci retracement enforcement

### Required Constraints:
- Price must remain within impulse structure
- Directional bias must remain intact
- No full reversal of impulse leg

---

## INSTITUTIONAL BEHAVIOR MODEL

The module interprets Mat Hold as:

### Bullish Case:
- Buyers absorb selling pressure during pullback
- No meaningful retracement of impulse leg
- Accumulation occurs inside consolidation

### Bearish Case:
- Sellers absorb buying pressure during pullback
- No meaningful rally against impulse leg
- Distribution occurs inside consolidation

---

## EVENT RULE ENGINE

The validation system processes real-time price action:

### SEED STATE RULES
- Break above structure → CONFIRM (bullish)
- Break below structure → CONFIRM (bearish)
- Opposite break → FAIL

### PENDING STATE RULES
- Same logic as SEED but with higher confidence threshold

### CONFIRMED STATE RULES
- Only invalidation occurs if structure is violated
  - Bullish FAIL: close below low
  - Bearish FAIL: close above high

### EXPIRATION RULE
- If `days_active > 15` → EXPIRE
